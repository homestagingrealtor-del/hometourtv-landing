Privacy Policy

Effective date: [To be set before publishing] · Last updated: July 2026

1. Who we are

Home Tour TV is operated by 2813434 Alberta Ltd., doing business as Olyveco, a corporation incorporated under the laws of Alberta, Canada. In this Privacy Policy, “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to 2813434 Alberta Ltd.

Home Tour TV is a Canadian YouTube channel dedicated to active property video tours. The submityourlisting.ca web application is the tool through which real estate agents and property owners submit listing content for publication on the channel.

2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information collected through:

  • hometourtv.com (the marketing landing page and waitlist)
  • submityourlisting.ca (the listing submission application)
  • The Olyveco ecosystem, including olyveco.com, where a user’s profile data may be shared across platforms

This policy does not apply to the Home Tour TV YouTube channel itself, which is operated on YouTube’s platform and subject to Google’s privacy policy.

3. Our legal obligations

We are subject to the following Canadian privacy laws:

  • PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act) — federal legislation governing how private-sector organizations collect, use, and disclose personal information in the course of commercial activities
  • PIPA (Personal Information Protection Act, Alberta) — provincial legislation applicable to our Alberta operations
  • CASL (Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation) — governing commercial electronic messages sent to users who sign up or submit listings

4. What personal information we collect

4.1 Information you provide directly

When you join the waitlist on hometourtv.com:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Province
  • Brokerage name (optional)

When you create an account on submityourlisting.ca:

  • Full name
  • Professional headshot or profile photo
  • Brokerage name and address
  • Phone number and email address
  • Province of operation
  • Website URL (optional)

When you submit an MLS listing:

  • MLS listing number and brokerage name
  • Property address (street address, city, province)
  • Listing price, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage
  • Property type and property age
  • Highlight text and full property description (optional)
  • Narration text (used to generate voice narration)
  • Voice type preference (female, neutral, or male)
  • Listing URL (optional, used for QR code)
  • Listing photos (up to 20, max 20 MB each) with per-photo captions

When you submit a private sale listing:

  • All of the above, except MLS number is replaced by title holder name (must match your account name)

4.2 Information we collect automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, referring URL
  • Google Analytics 4 data (see Section 9)

4.3 Information we derive or generate

  • Assembled listing videos from your submitted photos, data, and captions using Creatomate
  • AI-generated voice narration from your narration text using ElevenLabs text-to-speech
  • Video thumbnails generated as a snapshot of the video template
  • YouTube video metadata (title, description, tags) from your submission and profile data
  • QR codes linking to your listing URL or website

5. How we use your personal information

We use personal information to:

  • Produce branded listing video tours from your submitted photos, captions, and data
  • Generate AI voice narration from your narration text using ElevenLabs
  • Publish listing videos on the Home Tour TV YouTube channel
  • Generate video thumbnails with your name, photo, brokerage, and contact info
  • Embed your contact details in persistent video overlays
  • Process payments through Stripe for premium templates
  • Communicate with you about your account and listings
  • Analyse aggregate usage data to improve platform performance

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. How we share your personal information

6.1 Information published publicly on YouTube

When your listing video is published, the following becomes publicly visible: your full name, brokerage name, professional photo, phone number, email address, brokerage address, MLS number (or “Private Sale” designation), property address, listing price and details, photos, captions, AI-generated narration, and your listing URL as a QR code.

You acknowledge and consent to this public disclosure when you submit a listing.

7. Data retention

  • Waitlist signups: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or until 24 months of inactivity.
  • Accounts: Retained for the duration of your active relationship plus 7 years.
  • Listing records: Retained for 7 years following the listing’s removal date.
  • Published videos: Removed from YouTube when you mark the listing as sold through your dashboard, or upon request.
  • Analytics data: Retained per Google Analytics 4 settings (default: 14 months).

8. Your privacy rights

Under PIPEDA and PIPA, you have the right to access, correct, withdraw consent, and request deletion of your personal information. You may also file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (priv.gc.ca) or the OIPC Alberta (oipc.ab.ca).

We will respond to privacy requests within 30 days.

9. Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our platforms. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or retargeting.

10. Data security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), row-level security policies, access controls, and secure API authentication. Payment card data is processed by Stripe and never stored on our systems.

11. Cross-border data transfers

Our service providers may store or process your personal information outside of Canada, including in the United States. By using our platforms, you consent to such transfers.

12. Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email.
